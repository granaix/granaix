Granaix is a South Asia based dry agriculture produce export company specializing in the sourcing and global distribution of high-quality agricultural produce. Working closely with regional farmers and producers in South Asia, we ensure consistency, traceability, and strict quality control at every stage—from harvest and processing to packaging and export. Our product range includes carefully selected dry agricultural produce tailored to meet international standards, serving both the producer and the consumer simultaneously.


Our main goal is to bridge the global demand for dry agricultural produce with the expertise and dedication of skilled farmers and producers in South Asia, creating a seamless connection between local cultivation and international markets. By facilitating this link, we enable farmers and producers to expand their reach, grow sustainably, and participate in global trade, while ensuring that consumers worldwide receive responsibly sourced, premium-quality produce they can rely on for consistency and authenticity. Through careful sourcing and efficient logistics, we strive to provide high-quality produce, fostering trust and long-term relationships between producers and consumers.
