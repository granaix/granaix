WELCOME TO



Granaix is a South Asia–based export company specializing in premium dry agricultural produce. We partner closely with skilled farmers and producers to source and deliver high-quality, responsibly sourced products to consumers worldwide. From coffee and tea to spices and other dry goods, our mission is to provide exceptional produce that meets global standards, supports local farming communities, and satisfies the growing demand for trustworthy, high-quality agricultural produce.
